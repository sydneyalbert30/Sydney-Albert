HEALTH

Throughout my entire life, I have had stomach issues with certain foods. It was not until recently that I found a lifestyle that works for me, thanks to the help of my inspiration Jennifer Fisher. The "Jennifer Fisher Website" has absolutely changed my life. I am now gluten free, dairy free, soy free and grain free, and I have never felt better. Other than eating clean I have found workouts that I really enjoy, including pilates, cycling and yoga.
